Featured - Blackbird Pizza

Blackbird Pizzeria has been serving awesome vegan pizza in Philly since 2010. In addition, they offer an outstanding selection of sandwiches, wraps, salads, and vegan desserts. You can order by the pie or the slice, with takeout and delivery available as well. Our staff favorite pie is The Haymaker, with the South Philly running a close second. The Haymaker consists of crumbled seitan sausage, onions, garlic butter, and fresh oregano atop Daiya cheese. If Daiya doesn’t float your boat, we suggest the Tomato Pie topped with shaved elephant garlic and fresh herbs. This pizza really lets Blackbird’s crust shine: perfectly crisp on the outside and chewy on the inside.

For sandwiches we suggest the Seitan Cheesesteak. The thinly sliced seitan, grilled onions, bell peppers, and sautéed mushrooms combine to create the best vegan cheesesteak in the city.

Blackbird also serves Buffalo and BBQ wings and a selection of desserts from Vegan Treats. The wings are cornmeal battered and extra-crispy. Blackbird’s decor recalls a classic New York slice shop, but with hardcore punk coming out of the speakers. They might even be playing your favorite Minor Threat track when you arrive.
